The Foundation
CrossFit is constantly varied functional movement performed at high intensity.

It uses natural, full-body movements done with purpose. Intensity is scaled to every individual, making it safe, effective, and universally applicable.

The result: real-world fitness that prepares you for anything life throws your way.

Three Defining Principles
🎲
Constantly Varied
No two workouts are the same. Routine is the enemy.
⚙️
Functional Movement
Natural, compound movements your body was built to do.
⚡
High Intensity
Scaled to you. Power output drives results.
The CrossFit Prescription
WORLD-CLASS FITNESS
" Eat meat and vegetables, nuts and seeds, some fruit, little starch, and no sugar. Keep intake to levels that will support exercise but not body fat. Practice and train major lifts: Deadlift, clean, squat, presses, C&J, and snatch. Similarly, master the basics of gymnastics: pull-ups, dips, rope climbs, push-ups, sit-ups, presses to handstands, pirouettes, flips, splits, and holds. Bike, run, swim, row, etc. hard and fast. Five or six days per week mix these elements in as many combinations and patterns as creativity will allow. Routine is the enemy. Keep workouts short and intense. Regularly learn and play new sports.
— Greg Glassman, CrossFit Founder

Balance
Controlling the body's center of gravity relative to support base.

Accuracy
Controlling movement in a given direction or at a given intensity.
Training here at CrossFit OTL develops all of them, not just one.

⚡
Phosphagen
Maximum power output for very short bursts. Think: a 1-rep max lift or a 10-second all-out sprint. Burns phosphocreatine stored in muscle.
🔥
Glycolytic
Medium-duration, high-intensity work. Think: a 400m run or a fast MetCon. Burns glycogen — the "burn" you feel is this system at work.
🌿
Oxidative
Long, sustained aerobic effort. Think: a 5K run or a 20-minute row. Burns fat and carbohydrates with oxygen. Foundation of endurance.

A strong foundation is key. This pyramid shows the logical flow of an athlete's development — from foundational nutrition to advanced athletic expression.
