Service 01

BODY COMPOSITION

The Truth Mirror.

The scale lies. The InBody doesn't. It measures lean mass, fat mass, visceral fat, and water distribution — the metrics that actually predict how your body performs and ages.

  • See fat vs. muscle — not just weight
  • Scans every 4 weeks during the Reset (4 total)
  • Your first scan is free in the consultation
Service 02

1:1 PERSONAL TRAINING

Technique Before Intensity.

Required for every first-timer. No exceptions. You can't scale what isn't built right. 3 on-ramp sessions minimum, then 1:1 as needed.

  • Movement assessment and on-ramp
  • Benchmark testing
  • Skills development — the foundation for everything else
